Making it to the town hall

The twon hall is in the city center, so it may be hard to find a place to park (the car park inside the town hall is not open to the public) here are two tips:

If you are organized, you can print the map of parking spots in Dijon.

If you are already late, you can try to find a free spot in a parking lot on parking-dijon.com
