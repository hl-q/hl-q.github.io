Location for ceremony and cocktail

If the weather is nice and contrary to what has been written on the invitation, the ceremony and the cocktail will be held at Château d'Entre-Deux-Monts, 8 km away from Nuits-Saint-Georges. The meeting time is still 15h30.

In case of bad weather, the ceremony and the cocktail will be held at Maison de Nuits-Saint-Georges, 3 rue de la Berchère (see on Google Maps).

Car pooling

If you are looking for or offering a seat in a car to go to Château d'Entre-Deux-Monts, please let us know by email at mariage@hl-q.fr . We will connect you!

Itineraries

GPS corrdinates are 47.18101, 4.91984. If you don't find Château d'Entre-Deux-Monts in the address search of your GPS, please try with points of interest.

From Nuits-Saint-Georges

Take road D974 to Dijon. Drive to Vosne-Romanée. At the end of the village, turn left to Concœur et Corboin on road D109. Continue on road D109 (turn right in front of the church and turn left at the cemetery). After 3 kilometers, turn right at the crossroad with the stone cross to Château d'Entre-Deux-Monts. Continue to the Château and park outside the parc.

Click here to see the itinerary on Google Maps.

From Dijon

Via highway A31, Go to Nuits-Saint-Georges and follow the itinerary from Nuits-Saint-Georges (but you'd rather take road D974).

Take the road D974 to Nuits-Saint-Georges and drive to Vosne-Romanée. At the village entrance, turn right to Concœur et Corboin on road D109. Continue on road D109 (turn right in front of the church and turn left at the cemetery). After 3 kilometers, turn right at the crossroad with the stone cross to Château d'Entre-Deux-Monts. Continue to the Château and park outside the parc.

Warning : GPS can suggest you a shorter way that goes short before Vougeot, but that road is a rather impractical small path.
