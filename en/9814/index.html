Goal

Recompose an invitation card step by step. When two invitation cards at the same step meet, they collapse into an invitation card at the following step.

How to play?

In front of the camera, tilt the (paper) invitation card up, down, left, or right to make the tiles slide in this direction. Keyboard arrows works too.
